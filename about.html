Our Story

Siren Beauty + Wellness was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that beauty and wellness are not separate pursuits, but deeply interconnected aspects of a life well-lived.

As a woman-founded, locally owned boutique med spa, we've created a sanctuary in downtown Buda's historic district where cutting-edge aesthetic treatments meet mindful wellness practices. Our approach is rooted in science, elevated by artistry, and grounded in genuine care for each person who walks through our doors.

Our mission is to provide exceptional aesthetic care in an environment that feels less like a medical office and more like a restorative retreat. We're honored to serve our community and help each client discover their most confident, beautiful self.

Meet Our Team

Experienced practitioners dedicated to your beauty and wellness journey

Bex

Founder & Creative Director

Bex founded Siren Beauty + Wellness with a vision to create a sanctuary where aesthetic excellence meets holistic wellness. With over a decade of experience in the beauty and wellness industry, she brings a unique blend of clinical expertise and creative vision to every aspect of the spa.

Her philosophy centers on enhancing natural beauty through personalized, science-backed treatments delivered in a nurturing environment. Bex is passionate about empowering clients to feel confident and radiant in their own skin.

Skylar

Lead Esthetician

Skylar brings artistry and precision to every treatment she performs. As our lead esthetician, she specializes in advanced injectable techniques and has a gift for creating beautifully natural results that enhance each client's unique features.
