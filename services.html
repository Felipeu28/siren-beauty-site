Injectable Treatments

Neurotoxin Treatments

Our neurotoxin treatments (including Botox and Dysport) are the gold standard for smoothing fine lines and wrinkles. These FDA-approved injectables work by temporarily relaxing targeted facial muscles, resulting in a refreshed, youthful appearance.

What We Treat

  • Forehead lines and wrinkles
  • Frown lines between eyebrows (glabellar lines)
  • Crow's feet around the eyes
  • Bunny lines on the nose
  • Lip lines and gummy smile
  • Neck bands and jawline contouring

Treatment Details

  • Duration: 15-30 minutes
  • Results Appear: 3-7 days
  • Results Last: 3-4 months
  • Downtime: None to minimal
  • Pain Level: Minimal discomfort

Collagen Stimulator

Sculptra

Sculptra is a revolutionary poly-L-lactic acid (PLLA) injectable that works beneath the skin's surface to stimulate your body's natural collagen production. Unlike traditional fillers, Sculptra provides gradual, natural-looking results that can last up to two years.

Benefits of Sculptra

  • Restores facial volume lost due to aging
  • Improves skin texture and firmness
  • Softens deep facial folds and wrinkles
  • Creates subtle, natural-looking results
  • Long-lasting effects (up to 2+ years)
  • Stimulates natural collagen production

Ideal Treatment Areas

  • Cheeks and mid-face volume restoration
  • Nasolabial folds (smile lines)
  • Marionette lines
  • Temples
  • Jawline definition

Treatment Details

  • Duration: 30-45 minutes
  • Results Appear: Gradually over 2-3 months
  • Results Last: Up to 2+ years
  • Sessions Needed: Typically 2-3 sessions, 4-6 weeks apart
  • Downtime: Minimal; may have mild swelling for 2-3 days

Lip Enhancement

Our lip enhancement treatments use premium hyaluronic acid fillers to create beautifully balanced, natural-looking lips. We focus on enhancing your unique features while maintaining harmony and proportion with your facial structure.

What We Can Achieve

  • Add volume and fullness to thin lips
  • Improve lip symmetry and definition
  • Enhance the lip border (vermillion border)
  • Smooth vertical lip lines
  • Create a subtle "lip flip" effect
  • Restore volume lost with aging

Our Approach

We believe in enhancing, not overfilling. Our technique focuses on creating natural-looking results that complement your facial features. During your consultation, we'll discuss your goals and design a customized treatment plan that achieves your desired look while maintaining natural proportions.

Treatment Details

Before Your Treatment

  • Avoid blood-thinning medications and supplements (aspirin, ibuprofen, fish oil, vitamin E) for 7-10 days
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before treatment
  • Arrive with a clean face (no makeup)
  • Eat a light meal before your appointment
  • Inform us of any medications or allergies
  • Plan for minimal downtime, especially for filler treatments

After Your Treatment

  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Stay upright for 4 hours post-neurotoxin treatment
  • Apply ice to reduce swelling (for fillers)
  • Avoid touching or massaging treated areas
  • Stay hydrated and get adequate rest
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure and heat
  • Contact us with any concerns or questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are injectable treatments safe?

Yes, when performed by qualified practitioners. All our treatments use FDA-approved products and are administered by experienced, licensed professionals in a sterile clinical environment.

Will treatments look natural?

Absolutely. Our philosophy centers on enhancing your natural beauty, not changing who you are. We pride ourselves on subtle, natural-looking results that make you look refreshed, not overdone.

How long do results last?

Neurotoxins typically last 3-4 months, dermal fillers last 6-18 months depending on the product and area treated, and Sculptra results can last up to 2+ years.

Is there any downtime?

Most treatments require little to no downtime. You may experience mild swelling, redness, or bruising that typically resolves within a few days. Many clients return to normal activities immediately.

Do treatments hurt?

Most clients report minimal discomfort. We use ultra-fine needles and can apply topical numbing cream to ensure your comfort throughout the treatment.
